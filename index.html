We are HillView Ranch

A Wildlife Preserve and Working Cattle Ranch between Lockhart and Luling in Central Texas

Overview

THE RANCH CAN ACCOMODATE FROM A DOZEN TO OVER ONE HUNDRED PEOPLE FOR A GETAWAY, MEETINGS AND EVENTS. CHURCHES, CHARITIES AND SCHOOLS HAVE USED THE FACILITIES FOR "RANCH DAYS", SWIM PARTIES, STAFF RETREATS, BAPTISMS, WEDDINGS AND MORE.

Our Mission

"HillView Ranch is a gift from God that we are nurturing. We preserve both native and exotic wildlife, we provide education in the community on conservation and we raise cattle." Jane and Pat Durbin

Hays-Caldwell County Women's Center

The Hays-Caldwell Women's Center serves victims of family violence, sexual assault and child abuse in Caldwell County and Hays County in Central Texas.

HillView Ranch has had a long-term relationship with the center to offer a safe, comfortable and fun day out. On 'ranch days' the families fish, swim, picnic, hayride, learn, meet the donkeys and view the exotics.
